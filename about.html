Joyful Cooking is created by Mission Bit students. It's a non-enterprise website filled with recipes of different cuisines. Here at Joyful Cooking, we believe in the philosophy that cooking is the base of all human lives
and in many ways, a resemblance of life. Cooking is like an adventure, where we can choose whether or not
to step out of our comfort zones and attempt something new or even unheard of.
It takes precision and a lot of practice. Every mistake or failure is a lesson learned.
Cooking allows room for creativity, creating an environment where people can connect and learn from each other.
Our website is home to a variety of recipes, shared by a community of cooks and food experts around us.
